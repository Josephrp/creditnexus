# Responsible AI License (RAIL)

## CreditNexus Responsible AI Usage Guidelines

This Responsible AI License (RAIL) outlines the ethical guidelines and usage restrictions for CreditNexus, an algorithmic nature-finance platform that bridges legal contracts with ground truth verification.

### Permitted Uses

You may use CreditNexus for:

1. **Financial Services**: Legitimate financial services, loan origination, and credit analysis
2. **Compliance**: Regulatory compliance, audit trails, and policy enforcement
3. **Sustainability Verification**: Environmental monitoring and sustainability-linked loan verification
4. **Research**: Academic and commercial research in financial technology and environmental monitoring
5. **Document Processing**: Legal document extraction, analysis, and structured data conversion

### Prohibited Uses

You may NOT use CreditNexus for:

1. **Illegal Activities**: Any activity that violates applicable laws or regulations
2. **Fraud**: Financial fraud, identity theft, or deceptive practices
3. **Discrimination**: Discriminatory lending practices based on protected characteristics
4. **Market Manipulation**: Market manipulation, insider trading, or securities fraud
5. **Environmental Harm**: Activities that directly contribute to environmental degradation
6. **Privacy Violations**: Unauthorized collection or use of personal financial information
7. **Sanctions Evasion**: Circumventing international sanctions or embargoes
8. **Weaponization**: Use in weapons systems or military applications without explicit authorization

### Ethical Guidelines

When using CreditNexus, you agree to:

1. **Transparency**: Disclose AI-assisted decision-making where required by law
2. **Fairness**: Ensure algorithmic decisions do not perpetuate bias or discrimination
3. **Accountability**: Maintain audit trails and accountability for AI-driven decisions
4. **Privacy**: Protect personal and financial data in accordance with applicable privacy laws
5. **Environmental Responsibility**: Use the platform to promote sustainable finance practices
6. **Human Oversight**: Maintain appropriate human oversight of automated decisions

### Compliance Requirements

Users must:

- Comply with all applicable financial regulations (MiCA, Basel III, FATF, etc.)
- Adhere to data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- Maintain appropriate security measures for financial data
- Report any security vulnerabilities responsibly
- Respect intellectual property rights

### Enforcement

Violations of this RAIL may result in:

- Revocation of access to CreditNexus services
- Legal action where applicable
- Reporting to relevant regulatory authorities

### Reporting Violations

To report violations of this RAIL, please contact:
- Email: compliance@creditnexus.com
- Security: security@creditnexus.com

### Updates

This RAIL may be updated periodically. Continued use of CreditNexus after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
